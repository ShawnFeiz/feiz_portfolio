Hi, I'm Shawn! I'm a developer, tech enthusiast, parent's wifi fixer, and consultant based in Champaign Chicago, IL. I want to transform technology from something shiny, metallic, and foreign, into something personal, emotional, and human.

I'm currently a faculty consultant at the Kellogg School of Management, working on faculty and student experiences. While at Kellogg I was lucky enough to be part of Northwestern's Full Stack Coding Boot Camp. The program focused on the MERN stack with additional coverage on vanilla JS, jQuery, Firebase, SQL, git, handlebars etc.

I love to speak about current and future tech, especially on ways to make our lives easier using it. One of my passions is teaching people of all walks of life how to code! Currently I'm also mentoring colleagues on web technologies, languages, and development.

In my spare time, I enjoy cooking, traveling to different cities for unique tastes, and a cold pint of Guinness with my friends.
